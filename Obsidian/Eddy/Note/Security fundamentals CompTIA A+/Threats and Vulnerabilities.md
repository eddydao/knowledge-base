# Threats 
Anything that could cause harm, loss, damage, or compromise to information technology systems. eg: natural disasters, Cyber-attacks, Data integrity breaches, Disclosure of confidential information,…

# Vulnerabilities
 Any weakness in the system design or implementation
 eg: Software bugs, misconfigured software,…

> The job of cybersecurity is to manage the risk of those systems and networks => mitigating, transferring, avoiding or accepting the risks within those systems

Threat + No vulnerability = No risk
No Threat + Vulnerability = No risk

## Types of Vulnerabilities

1. Vulnerability of a lack of preparation
2. Scheduling Vulnerability
3. Vehicular Vulnerability
4. …

> The threats will mostly outside of control

# What we can do
Risk management is finding different ways to minimize the likelihood of an outcome occurring and achieve the desired outcomes
